JOIN US! We are the answer

Passion Drives Us

Here at LASD, we pride ourselves in what we do and carry out our work everyday with a sense of love and duty. For out of this mentality, we've become a team of hard (and smart) working professionals who rely and learn from each other, and who, in the direst of situations and tightest of deadlines, always get the job done and grow from that experience. If this sounds like a crew you can vibe with, we at LASD, implore you to join our growing team of passionate GIS professionals. And if that hasn't convinced you, kindly glance over the benefits we offer.

Benefits

Here at LASD we understand that you're an emotional being with needs. Although we love our work, WE are MORE than our WORK. In order to keep our employees working for us at maximum efficiency, we do our best to take care of their physical and mental health.

Health Care

We offer an comprehensive health care program which includes travel insurance, chiropractic, dental, vision, disability accessibility and injury remediation to all full-time employees. These benefits extend to family and any dependants. We care about our employees ability to work for us at maximum efficiency, since as we have learned from experience when straining to meet a last-minute deadline, health should be the LASD thing on your mind.

Work-Life Balanace

We offer flexible working hours and five weeks of paid vacation per year in order to get that well deserved break. This is done with hopes of encouraging our team members to live beyond their work and contribute to good in this world. We also cover the costs for further education and professional development that team members wish to pursue in order to bring back and contribute to our mission. Here at LASD, we believe in growing our collective skillset to remain relevant in the ever-evolving technical landscape that is GIS.

Recreation

Nothing builds that sense of comraderie better than engaging in fun, team-bonding activities. Here at LASD, we commit our team members to take part in weekly team exercies and sports such as Escape Rooms, recreation sport leagues (Soccer, Basketball, Hockey), rock climbing, archery tag, paintball and so much more.

Convenient Finances

Here at LASD, you're entitled to annual salary increases of up to 20 percent. Additionally, we offer bi-weekly RRSP contributions via a collaborative retirement savings program to facilitate those golden years we all yearn for.
